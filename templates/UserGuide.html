Comprehensive Password Manager User Guide

1. Introduction

Discover the power of the Comprehensive Password Manager: your one-stop solution for enhanced password security and management.
Our web application, designed to work on a range of systems, aims to streamline the process of creating and organizing your passwords. With a range of encryption
algorithms to choose from, your passwords remain safeguarded against potential threats. Rest easy knowing that your passwords are stored in an encrypted database, impervious
to unauthorized access even in the face of a compromised database. Through a user-friendly GUI interface, effortlessly create, encrypt, modify, and delete passwords. Elevate your
password security while enjoying unparalleled convenience with the Comprehensive Password Manager.

2. User Registration and Authentication

Creating a New Account:

  • Click on "Create Account."
  • Fill out the registration form with your information.
  • Choose a strong master password. Follow the guidelines provided for a secure password.
  • Click "Submit" to create your account.

Logging In:

  • Navigate to the login page.
  • Enter your username and master password.
  • Click "Login."

Forgot Password:

  • On the login page, click on "Forgot Password."
  • Follow the instructions to reset your master password securely.
  • Once reset, you can log in with your new password.

3. Navigating the User Interface

Dashboard Overview:

The dashboard displays all your password entries. Each entry shows the account name, username, and last modified date.

Menu Navigation:

Use the menu to access different sections of the application. You can navigate between Home, Password Generation, Password Manager, Privacy Page, and User Guide.

4. Password Management

Generating Strong Passwords:

  • After logging in, navigate to the password generator.
  • Choose your preferences (length, uppercase, lowercase, mixed case, numbers, symbols).
  • Click "Generate" to receive a strong password.

Adding a New Password Entry:

  • Access your dashboard.
  • Click "Add New Password."
  • Enter the necessary information (account name, username, URL, etc.).
  • Choose an encryption algorithm for the password.
  • Enter the password generated or your custom password.
  • Click "Save."

Modifying an Existing Password:

  • Find the password entry you want to modify.
  • Click on the entry to access its details.
  • Edit the information as needed.
  • Click "Save Changes."

Deleting a Password Entry:

  • Locate the password entry you wish to delete.
  • Click on the entry to view its details.
  • Click "Delete".
  • Confirm the deletion when prompted.

5. Encryption Options

Choosing an Encryption Algorithm:

When adding or modifying a password, choose an encryption algorithm from the provided options. Each algorithm offers different levels of security. Read the descriptions to decide.

How Encryption Works:

When you save a password, it is encrypted using the chosen algorithm. Even if the database is compromised, the passwords remain secure.

6. Troubleshooting and FAQs

Troubleshooting:

If you encounter any issues, check the following solutions:

  • Unable to log in:
    • Make sure you are using the correct username and master password. If you've forgotten your password, follow the steps in the "Forgot Password" section to reset it. If you entered your information incorrectly too many times, your account may be locked. Follow the information provided to reset your password and unlock your account access.
  • Passwords not appearing in the dashboard:
    • Check that you are logged in with the correct account. If you are using the correct account, try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, please contact us for further support.
  • Password entry modifications not saving:
    • Make sure you click “Save Changes” after editing a password entry. Check your internet connection, as changes require an active connection to be saved.
  • Application not loading properly:
    • Clear your browser’s cache and cookies, then try accessing the application again. If the problem persists, try using a different web browser.

7. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are answers to some common questions:

  • How do I change my master password?
    • To change your master password, log in to your account and navigate to the "Settings" section. Look for the "Change Master Password" option and follow the prompts to set a new secure password.
  • Can I recover my account if I forget my master password and recovery question?
    • Unfortunately, we cannot recover your account if you forget your master password and recovery questions. This is due to the strong encryption measures in place to protect your data. Make sure to keep your master password and recovery question secure.
  • Is my data accessible to anyone else?
    • No, your data is stored securely using advanced encryption algorithms. Only you have access to your data with your master password.
  • Can I use the Comprehensive Password Manager on multiple devices?
    • Yes, you can access the application from different devices as long as you have an internet connection and a compatible web browser.
  • What happens if I close my account?
    • If you choose to close your account, all your data will be permanently deleted. Make sure to export any important passwords before closing your account.
